About MySlam



what is slam book?


It's a place to document the things (music, foods, boys, etc.) you and your friends dig. Here's a real one, in its entirety, from 1982.

A slam book is a notebook which is passed among people. The keeper of the book starts by posting a question (which may be on any subject) and the book is then passed round for each contributor to fill in their own answer to the question.

Slam books were popular among many students. Each adding their own comment about a certain person, subject, ... etc.



What/Why MySlam?


MySlam is a social site that connects people with your organization, college, institutions, hotel, banks etc. This will help to take feedback from user at the time when students are passed out, people faced retirements and so on.

MySlam also works as business evolution model. Any business organization, college, hotel, banks, showroom, mall can grow their productivity and services by knowing that, what actually users(client, students, customers, etc.) think about theirr particular organization.

Entertainment
